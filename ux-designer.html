Product Design UX & UI

My specialties include product definition, designs and working on high traffic, high volume websites and applications. I thrive when it's not clear what the solution is, and the best way forward is to prototype fast and validate along the way. It is important to deeply understand the user journey, conduct interviews and usability tests. It is also important to work with product managers and business analysts to interate on design and flow.

Design Thinking & UX Process Commonalities in bold. Highlights general process. Real world solutions vary based on project needs. There is no one size fits all.

Design Thinking Process
  1. Empathize to gain understanding of the problem we are trying to solve.
  2. Define user needs, problems, insights
  3. Ideate challenge assumptions, creative ideas for innovation
  4. Prototype start creating solutions
  5. Test the solutions
UX Process
  1. Ideate Concept brainstorm, gather and analyze information.
  2. Scenario storyboarding. Map the user journey. Create personas
  3. Wireframe / Design
  4. Prototype
  5. Share / Test / Feedback
  6. Conduct design reviews.
  7. Iterate. Build.
